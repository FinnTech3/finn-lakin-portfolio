Executive Summary

Finn's Strategy is a proprietary quantitative trading system designed to trade S&P 500 E-mini futures (ES) across multiple market regimes simultaneously. Unlike single-strategy systems that break down in changing conditions, this framework runs eleven distinct sub-strategies in parallel — from ICT Smart Money concepts to macro-driven positioning — and uses an adaptive synthesis layer powered by Claude API to dynamically weight each strategy based on the detected market regime.

A three-month forward test on $10,000 starting capital returned +156% across seven trades with an 86% win rate. The framework's largest contributor was a short position entered ahead of the Iran war escalation in Q1 2026, generating +91% on that position alone. The second largest was a long at the S&P 200-day moving average using ICT Optimal Trade Entry methodology, returning +36%.
