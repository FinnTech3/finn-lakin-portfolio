Privacy Notice

Last updated: May 2026  ·  Version 1.0

Who this covers

This notice applies to visitors to finnlakin.com (this site) and anyone who contacts me through it. It is a personal portfolio site; there is no company behind it.

What data this site collects

This site does not use cookies, tracking pixels, or third-party analytics. It does not record your IP address, browsing behaviour, or any personally identifiable information by default.

The only browser storage used is localStorage (not a cookie) to remember your chosen colour palette (Brass on Black or FT Pink) between visits. This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted anywhere.

If you contact me via email, phone, or LinkedIn using the details on this site, any information you share will be used solely to respond to your message.

Embedded content & third-party services

This site loads the following third-party resources:

  • Google Fonts (fonts.googleapis.com / fonts.gstatic.com) — loads typeface files. Google may log requests in accordance with their privacy policy.
  • jsDelivr CDN (cdn.jsdelivr.net) — serves the Chart.js library. jsDelivr may log CDN requests per their policy.
  • Alpaca / finance demo — the optional live finance demo runs on a local server on your own machine; no data is sent to a remote server unless you explicitly choose to deploy it.

No data from those third-party requests is accessible to me.

Hosting & server logs

When the site is deployed, the hosting provider (e.g., Cloudflare, Render, or similar) may retain standard server access logs (IP address, URL, timestamp) for security and abuse-prevention purposes. Those logs are governed by the provider's own privacy policy and are not accessible to me in identifiable form.

Your rights

Because this site does not collect personal data, there is nothing stored about you to access, correct, or erase. If you believe otherwise, or have any privacy concern, contact me at lakin.finn@gmail.com and I will respond within a reasonable time.

Changes to this notice

If this site begins using analytics or any data-collecting service in the future, this notice will be updated before that service goes live and a new "last updated" date will appear at the top.
